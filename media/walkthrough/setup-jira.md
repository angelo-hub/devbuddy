# Connect Jira Cloud - Guided Setup

Click the **"Start Jira Setup"** button below to begin a step-by-step guided setup!

## What You'll Need

1. **Any Jira URL**
   - Just paste any URL from your Jira!
   - Examples:
     - A ticket URL: `https://mycompany.atlassian.net/browse/ENG-123`
     - Your Jira homepage: `https://mycompany.atlassian.net`
     - Any page from your Jira
   - DevBuddy will automatically extract your site URL

2. **Your Jira Email**
   - The email you use to log into Jira

3. **API Token**
   - Don't have one? No problem!
   - The setup wizard will guide you to create one
   - Takes just 30 seconds at [id.atlassian.com](https://id.atlassian.com/manage-profile/security/api-tokens)
