# Update Ticket Status

Keep your Linear board up-to-date without leaving VS Code. Change ticket status with a single click!

## How to Change Status

### Method 1: Context Menu
Right-click any ticket in the sidebar and select **"Change Ticket Status"**

### Method 2: Inline Button
Click the **$(edit)** icon that appears on hover in the sidebar

### Method 3: Ticket Detail Panel
Open any ticket and use the status dropdown at the top

## Workflow States

Linear Buddy shows all workflow states from your team's configuration:

- **$(layers) Backlog** - Not yet started
- **$(circle-outline) Todo** - Ready to start
- **$(play-circle) In Progress** - Currently working
- **$(check-all) Done** - Completed
- **$(circle-slash) Canceled** - No longer needed
- **$(milestone) Triage** - Needs review

The available states match your team's Linear workflow, so you'll see exactly what your team uses.
